Recipe for Mie Ayam

Description

Mie ayam is a common Indonesian dish of seasoned yellow wheat noodles topped with diced chicken meat. It especially popular in Indonesia and has been recognized as one of the top 10 most delicious food in the world.

The dish is made of yellow wheat noodles, boiled and then topped with diced chicken meat seasoned in sweet soy sauce, garlic, and pepper. It is usually served with wonton dumplings, bakso (meatballs), and a bowl of chicken broth.

Ingredients

  • 250 gr mince chicken (or cut small)
  • 2-3 shallot peel and slices thinly
  • 2 tsp mince garlic (or more if you like)
  • 1-2 tbsp kecap manis (This is fine to skip, but you will have savoury rather than sweet taste)
  • 2 tsp xylitol/sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce for chicken, 1 tbsp for the rest
  • 100 ml water

Cooking Instruction

  1. Heat the wok, adda bit of oil and stry fry the mince chicken first. add bit of soya sauce and white pepper till cooked, set it aside to the end bit of the wok

  2. Add a bit more oil (and some skin chicken oil if you use this), put the shallot and garlic still fry till fragrant. Mixed well. Add chillies if you used it. add the 1tbsp soya sauce, xylitol, oyster sauce, mixed and add the kecap manis and the water. put the heat to medium-low and let it simmer till the water evaporates.

  3. That would take about 10-15 minutes, then add the white pepper and the spring onion. taste it, you can add some salt if you need to. this is finish. This should be only has little liquid left (I sometimes used the liquid for mixing the noodle). -- you can freeze what you cant finish here.
